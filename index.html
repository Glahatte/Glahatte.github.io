Hello! My name is Grayson LaHatte.

College Career

Born and raised in Augusta Georgia, I attended Aquinas High School and subsequently Augusta University in which I graduated with a degree in Political Science with a focus on counter-terrorism and security.

During my time at Augusta Univeristy I joined several clubs, and was a member of the Delta Chi Fraternity. I held numerous officer positions in the chapter and assisted in consistently doubling the chapter size every year for three years straight.

Computer History

I started working with computers at a young age and would build my first working desktop when I was sixteen. I worked for months at a local resturant to save up enough money to build "Thor". I received the last of my parts on Christmas Day and spent the majority of my Christmas afternoon completing my first rig.

The excitment of powering on my computer for the first time, along with the "fun" of troubleshooting issues that popped up during the building process was remarkable. I was extremely proud of myself for having built Thor all by myself (with the help of some Youtube videos and Reddit guides as well)

After that day my love of computers was solidified. Thor fought the good fight for seven long years but eventually he became outdated. I donated Thor to my cousin who was a fan of PC gaming but did not have a PC himself. He was extremely excited and to this day is one of my favorite memories passing on my old computer to my cousin.

Life After Graduation

After graduating from Augusta University I began job searching. Despite my best efforts the job market for political science majors was not as strong as I was hoping it would be, with no desire to go to law school after working in a law firm for several years and witnessing first hand the stress that being a lawyer is I decided to go back to school, though I wasn't sure for what.

I considered getting my masters degree in security studies, but another two years of school without a full-time job was not on my agenda. As much as I love Augusta I have lived here for twenty-five years. It was time for me to move on, and move out.

Getting Started With the Clubhou.se

As I was attending a job fair hosted at the Georgia Cyber Center I met several members of TheClubHouse who told me about their full stack web development bootcamp. I was immediatly interested and registered for the course.

We started class in early september, and here we are! I have learned a lot in the past month alone and am extremely excited for the future.
